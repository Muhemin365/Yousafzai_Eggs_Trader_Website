About Yousafzai EGRO

Connecting Farms to Businesses, With Compliance at the Core

Yousafzai EGRO unites specialist egg trading with compliance-first B2B supply — bridging high-quality Pakistani farms and the businesses that depend on consistent, fully documented egg supply.

"To make egg supply predictable, sustainable, and profitable for every partner in our ecosystem — certified, traceable, and accountable at every step."

Founded in 2010 in Peshawar, Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, Yousafzai EGRO has grown into a specialist egg trading and B2B supply house — serving hotels, restaurants, bakeries, retailers, manufacturers and institutions with one unified, dependable source.

We believe the egg supply chain in Pakistan can be more efficient, more transparent, and more equitable for everyone — from the farmer to the final commercial buyer. That belief shapes a single-minded focus on documentation, compliance infrastructure, and account services built for serious B2B buyers.

Today, our trading and supply operation moves over 500,000 eggs weekly through a network of 40+ vetted partner farms, supported by a dedicated cold-chain fleet and three regional distribution hubs.

Our Process

A Documented, Auditable Process at Every Stage

01
Collection
Eggs collected from certified farms within 6 hours of laying. Each collection logged with farm ID, flock ID and timestamp.
02
Laboratory Testing
Salmonella swabs, shell strength, interior quality (Haugh units) and yolk color measured on every batch.
03
Candling & Grading
Automated candling detects cracks, blood spots and internal defects. Eggs are graded by size and shell quality.
04
Marking & Coding
Each egg is stamped with a batch code linking to the full digital audit trail accessible via our client portal.
05
Packing
Packed under hygienic conditions in client-specified or standard packaging, with batch certs included in every box.
06
Cold-Chain Delivery
Refrigerated delivery with IoT temperature logging. Delivery receipts include full chain-of-custody sign-off.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I receive a formal B2B quote?
Submit a quote request and our commercial team responds with a formal quotation within 4 business hours. Contracts can be activated within 3 working days.
What is the minimum order quantity?
Minimum orders vary by product — from 50 trays/week for organic eggs up to 500 trays/week for standard Grade A white shell eggs. Liquid whole egg starts at 500kg/week.
Which certifications and standards do you comply with?
All products meet ISO 22000:2018, PSQCA, and PNAC-accredited Halal standards, alongside active Animal Welfare, Environmental Audit, and HACCP certifications.
Can I trace a specific delivered batch?
Yes. Every box carries a batch code — scan it or look it up in the client portal to see farm collection, lab testing, grading, dispatch and delivery timestamps.
How does the Partner Program pricing work?
Pricing is tiered by weekly volume — Silver (100–499 trays), Gold (500–1,999 trays), and Platinum (2,000+ trays) — each unlocking deeper discounts and additional services.
Which cities and regions do you deliver to?
Our distribution hubs in Lahore, Karachi, and Peshawar serve 25+ cities directly, with next-day delivery at a 98% success rate.
